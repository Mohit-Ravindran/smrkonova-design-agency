Our Branding Process for Bangalore Businesses

Discovery Phase

We learn about your business vision, values, target customers, and competitors. Understanding your market position shapes the creative direction.

Strategic Planning

Based on research, we develop a brand strategy defining your positioning, messaging, and visual direction. Strategy comes before design.

Concept Development

We create multiple logo concepts exploring different creative approaches. Each concept comes with rationale explaining the thinking behind it.

Feedback and Refinement

You review concepts and provide feedback. We refine the selected direction through multiple rounds until the design is perfect.

Final Design and Assets

Once approved, we prepare all final files in various formats. You receive vector files, PNG, JPEG, and any other formats you need.

Brand Guidelines Creation

We document how to use your new brand correctly. The guidelines become your reference for maintaining consistency.

Implementation Support

We help apply your new brand across existing materials – website, social media, stationery, and marketing materials.
