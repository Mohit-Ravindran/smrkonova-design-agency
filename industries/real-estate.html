02The Shift

The Way People Buy Property Has Changed.

In 2025 and 2026, AI-powered search, online consultations, digital appointment booking, WhatsApp communication and Google reviews became a normal part of choosing Real Estate providers.

buyers increasingly compare projects online before they ever speak to a receptionist. projects that invest only in buildings and equipment — but not in their digital experience — risk losing buyers before the first interaction.

This isn't just about having a website anymore. It's about building trust before the buyer arrives.
